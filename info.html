Why do we need water?

  • Roughly 60% of our bodies are water.
  • Most American drink under or around 2.5 cups a day of water.
  • Women should roughly drink 12 cups a day and men should drink around 15 cups a day.

Dehydration can cause many health issues, from headaches to worsened heart rate. Riggs, Ben. “You’re Probably Not Drinking Enough Water.” Kettering Health, 11 Dec. 2023, ketteringhealth.org/youre-probably-not-drinking-enough-water/.
